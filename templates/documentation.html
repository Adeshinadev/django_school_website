How to use Prestige School Management System.

Welcome, this is the official documentation of how to use Prestige College school management system works. Please read this documentation meticulously as it’ll aid you navigate around the website easily, and give you a quick overview of how the components works together. (This system was built by the Lead Python-Django Developer; Aina Adeshina Daniel. At CoderHolic.)

Features of the Website


  • This site Displays Dynamic information to its visitor i.e. it displays updated information as directed by the manager, this implies that the manager can insert, update and delete information on the website. Examples of the information the site displays include; Latest news, upcoming events. Etc.
  • The website is programmed to give room for enquiries, i.e. users could log their complains and request by filling a Request Information Form, and this form will be submit to the admin’s/manager’s Dashboard for reply.
  • Student/Staff could also pay fees online.by the use of a reliable Payment Gateway (paystack) student, staff or any other users could pay any sort of fee on this website by just supplying their bank details. User could also print their receipt on the website.
  • Staff/teacher can upload student results on this website, hence student can view and print their results on the website.
  • Student and staffs, can upload and view their Biodata.
  • Lastly the website generates ID card for the registered students and staff.
